Start Stream via VLC GUI
1. Open VLC
2. Click "Media"
3. Select "Stream"

Streaming context menue:
1. Choose your mp4-File and click "Stream"
2. Click "Next"
3. Add RTP / MPEG Transport Stream and click "Next"
	* Address: 239.255.0.1	--> Random multicast IP address
	* Port: 5004
4. Deactivate transcoding and hit "Next"
5. Start stream with "Stream"

Click on repeat for continguos streaming the video

Receive Stream via VLC GUI
1. Open VLC
2. Click "Media"
3. Open network stream
4. Enter "rtp://@239.255.0.1:5004"
